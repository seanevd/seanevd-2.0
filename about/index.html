I’m Sean Duncan. I am a front-end developer who enjoys many aspects of product development, including product design and illustration. I currently enjoy having a 60/40 split in development and design, respectively. (I spend a majority of my time writing code.) I generally design abstract tools or workflows and put those designs into production code. I’ve worked with all sorts of tools including Angular, Meteor, React, jQuery, Gulp, Sass, Sketch, Photoshop, Illustrator, and much more.

I joined Nutrislice as the first front-end developer and subsequently their first designer as well. I always had a respect for good design but never had to advocate for it until I started there.

Previously, I studied both Mechanical and Electrical engineering, but ended up with a business degree. I ultimately decided to follow my passion of web development and have never looked back.
