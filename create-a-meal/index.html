Nutrislice Meal Planner (Create-a-Meal) is a web application that allows students and parents the ability to plan out their meals for the week or month. Users are able to view nutritional information based on their daily caloric need and can see how a meal stacks up against the federal myPlate requirements. Users select their meals, then can save and print their individualized plan.

Main view for the desktop view of Create-a-Meal.

I was given the task of designing the web app from the start. After several rounds of design critiques and feedback, a spec was nailed down. Immediately after design I built the tool alongside another develoer. I made the Nutrislice Meal planner as a fully responsive web app, it was incredibly difficult to reuse a majority of the elements for large and small screens sizes while maintaining a mobile app look and feel for the smaller screens.
